About me

Hello! My name is Perdiansyah, and I am the Founder of Syah Studio. I am a webcomic artist dedicated to the art of visual and narrative storytelling. With several years of experience, I have produced a diverse range of works.

Specializing in fantasy and adventure with a dramatic twist. Known for unpredictable plots, unique character designs, and immersive environments that bring every story to life.

I am currently developing my latest project, a webcomic titled 'Child of Fate'. This series represents my newest venture into the realms of Action Fantasy.
