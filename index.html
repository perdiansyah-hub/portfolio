Hello! My name is Perdiansyah, and I am the Founder of Syah Studio. I am a webcomic artist dedicated to the art of visual and narrative storytelling. With several years of experience, I have produced a diverse range of works.

Specializing in fantasy and adventure with a dramatic twist. Known for unpredictable plots, unique character designs, and immersive environments that bring every story to life.

I am currently developing my latest project, a webcomic titled 'Child of Fate'. This series represents my newest venture into the realms of Action Fantasy.

Get to know the characters in the "Child of Fate" story

DARIUS

Darius Agathinus

The Chosen One, destined to unite the world

An ordinary human who possesses extraordinary courage and the authority of a king. His journey begins when he discovers his divine destiny to unite the fractured realms against the coming darkness.

AGATHA

Agatha Aelia

The Mystic Mage, angel of destiny

An angel destined by the gods to change a predetermined fate. She becomes the companion of King Darius Agathinus, connected by divine destiny to spend her life with him and guide his path.

KAEL

Kael Dien

The God of Death

A god of death with the power to erase eternal existence, even among gods themselves. His motives remain shrouded in mystery as he walks the line between destruction and necessary balance.

SERAPHINA

Seraphina

The Ancient Guardian

An ancient guardian who has lived for thousands of years. She holds knowledge of prophecies and possesses the key to unlocking the mysteries of the past that will determine the future of all realms.

ZEPHYR

Zephyr

The Wind Spirit

A wind spirit who faithfully accompanies Darius. Though unable to speak, it communicates through movements of the wind and becomes a true companion in the adventure, offering guidance when needed most.
