My name is Perdiansyah, a webtoon artist and digital illustrator with over five years of experience creating fantasy-based visual storytelling

I founded Syah Studio as a dedicated space to develop original stories and illustrations. Through this studio, I aim to create narratives that feel alive — worlds with history, characters with real flaws, and stories that move beyond conventional fantasy tropes.

Alongside my creative work, I am currently pursuing a degree in Islamic Economics. This academic background has shaped how I approach creative projects, particularly in terms of sustainability, long-term development, and building a structured creative career.

My long-term goal is to reach a broader international audience. I am actively building my portfolio and developing original series to move toward that direction.

Currently, my main project is Child of Fate. an action-fantasy webtoon series that represents the core of my artistic vision and storytelling style.

Five years in, here's what I'm good at and how I approach each one.

Digital Illustration

I move between painterly and clean line-art depending on what the piece needs. The approach changes — getting the mood right doesn't.

Procreate · Clip Studio · Photoshop

Character Design

I start with silhouette — if it doesn't read from across the room, the details don't matter yet. Then I build inward until the character feels like someone specific.

Reference study · Turnarounds · Expression sheets

Sequential Storytelling

Writing and drawing my own stories means the pacing decisions happen together. I know when a panel needs space and when cutting away does more than showing everything.

Storyboarding · Panel layout · Script writing

World Building

I like worlds that feel like they've been around longer than the story. I work out the history and geography before drawing a single scene, so the details add up instead of contradict.

Lore writing · Environment design · Map-making

Webtoon Production

I handle the full pipeline — script to final upload. Vertical scroll format changes how you stage scenes, and I account for that from the very first thumbnail.

Webtoon format · Batch production · Color theory

Creative Marketing

Good work that nobody sees is still a problem. I think about how a project presents itself — the identity, the tone, how it finds the right audience.

Social media · Brand identity · Content strategy
